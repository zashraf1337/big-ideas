# Big Ideas — week ending 2026-05-15

A heavy commercialization week. **Bio** drove most of the signal: Alphabet-backed Isomorphic Labs closed a **$2.1B Series B** (Thrive Capital lead) — one of the largest private AI-drug-discovery rounds on record — while Intellia opened a **rolling BLA for lonvo-z**, the first-ever in vivo CRISPR product, with US launch tracking H1 2027 after Phase 3 HAELO showed an 87% reduction in HAE attacks. At ASGCT 2026, Editas reported a single dose of **EDIT-401** achieving ≥90% mean LDL-C reduction in NHPs (direct shot at Verve/Lilly's cardio gene-editing lane). Viking's oral **VK2735** hit 12.2% mean weight loss at 13 weeks at ECO 2026. And Neuralink signaled a **next-gen "whole-brain" surgical robot** as it scales toward high-volume implants.

**AI/robotics** matched the pace: Waymo expanded robotaxi coverage to **1,400 sq mi across 11 cities** (FIFA-readiness rollout); Figure ran a **full 8-hour fully autonomous package-sorting shift** on Helix-02; China's **ROBOTERA closed >$200M** led by SF Group/HSG/IDG to scale logistics deliveries; Google launched **Gemini Intelligence** as an OS-level agentic layer for Android; and Google added **Marvell** as a third TPU partner for ~2M inference MPUs. On the **energy/quantum** side: **IonQ–SkyWater** $1.8B merger cleared its shareholder vote (first vertically integrated quantum platform with in-house US fab), **CATL** announced a 40 GWh sodium-ion expansion, **BNEF confirmed the "100 GW era"** for energy storage in 2025 (112 GW / 307 GWh installed; +48% YoY), and **Ford** launched a 20 GWh/yr LFP grid-storage subsidiary out of the dissolved BlueOval SK plant. Agent D's broad scan turned up three notable adjacencies: Archer Aviation entering the UAE Restricted Type Certificate pathway (first eVTOL), the EU AI Act "omnibus" simplification (with a new nudification ban), and HKU's "SS-H2" ultra-stainless steel that could cut seawater-electrolysis structural costs ~40×.
